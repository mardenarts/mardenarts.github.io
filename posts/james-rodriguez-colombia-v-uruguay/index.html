James Rodriguez (Colombia v Uruguay)

Posted on 14 June 2015

James Rodríguez and the most beautiful goal from World Cup 2014. This goal also gave him the Puskas awards in 2014. A more complex scene to recreate, with a lot of players of Colombia and Uruguay to be synchronised. The most challenger here was to create the ball movement, as it bounces several times until it his the goal. I hope you all like the result. I personally like the audio a lot for the commentator passion. Comments credits to Tato Sanín and Caracol Radio in Colombia.
